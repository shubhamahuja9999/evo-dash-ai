✔️This shifts the focus to the recipient, making the email more relevant to them.




👉Skip Adverbs: 
✔️Words like ""just,"" ""fast,"" ""soon,"" and others can weaken your message. Be direct and assertive.

✔️Phrases like ""I think,"" ""I believe,"" or ""we offer solutions"" dilute your message. Be confident and specific.

👉Overpersonalization: While personalization is critical, avoid overdoing it. Personalization should feel natural, not forced.
